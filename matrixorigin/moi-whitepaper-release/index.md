### GenAI Challenges: Integration and Management of Multimodal Data

As GenAI becomes widely adopted, the value of unstructured and multimodal data has gained unprecedented recognition. However, managing and utilizing such data remains a critical obstacle for enterprises implementing AI applications:

- **Severe Data Fragmentation**: Enterprise data is scattered across multiple storage systems, making cross-format and cross-modal integration highly complex.
- **Complex Processing Workflows**: Non-structured data processing pipelines—from collection and parsing to storage and feature extraction—are lengthy and intricate.
- **Inadequate Intelligence**: Traditional tools fail to sufficiently support multimodal data preprocessing and optimization required for AI model training and inference.
- **High Real-Time Demands**: Increasingly dynamic business requirements impose stringent real-time constraints on data processing.

MatrixOne Intelligence addresses these challenges through its unique technical architecture and functional design, enabling enterprises to transform data into a powerful driver for GenAI implementation.
