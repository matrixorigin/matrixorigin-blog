To help AI enthusiasts better experience the powerful capabilities of ComfyUI, we are launching the "ComfyUI + 3090 GPU Compute Card Trial Event." We sincerely invite everyone interested in AI model training and debugging to participate. This event not only provides participants with rich learning resources, but also offers the opportunity to receive RTX 3090 compute power support, showcase AI skills, and win generous prizes.

### Event Benefits

- **High-performance compute power support**: Use the powerful compute power of RTX 3090 for free and experience an efficient model training process.

- **ComfyUI beginner tutorial**: Professional beginner tutorials help you quickly master model debugging skills.

- **Generous prizes**: Multiple awards are waiting for you. Showcase your AI technical capabilities and win the recognition you deserve.

### Event Process

#### Step 1: Register for the event

1. Registration period: From now until October 29, 2024.

2. Registration method: Click the [registration link](https://neolink-ai.com/oidc/?sns=comfyui) and submit your registration information.

3. Eligibility: Individuals interested in ComfyUI and AI model debugging are welcome to register. Users with basic AI knowledge or a development background are encouraged to participate.

#### Step 2: Register a Neolink.AI platform account and claim a 3090 trial voucher

1. Account verification: Complete real-name verification on the Neolink.AI platform.

2. Claim the trial voucher: After real-name verification is completed, the system will automatically issue a "1-hour 3090 compute power trial voucher" to each participant. With this voucher, participants can use RTX 3090 compute power for model training and debugging on the Neolink.AI platform for free, and experience cutting-edge high-performance compute power support.

#### Step 3: Submit debugging results, upload an explainer video and generated images

1. Learn ComfyUI: Follow the beginner tutorial provided for ComfyUI to learn how to use ComfyUI for AI model debugging and training. Click the [learning link](https://neolink-ai.com/docs/Built-in_tools/ComfyUI) to start learning.

2. Submit the debugged model and training results:

   - Use the provided 3090 compute power trial voucher to train and debug models on the Neolink.AI platform, and record the parameters and steps used during the training process.

   - Package the debugged model and its training results, and upload them to the designated email address.

3. Requirements for the explainer video and generated images:

   - Explainer video: Record and upload a 2-10 minute video showing your model debugging process, the problems encountered and their solutions, and share your training results.

   - Generated images: Use ComfyUI to generate high-quality images and upload them together. The images should highlight the generation quality and strengths of the AI model.

4. Submission method: Participants must send the four required items, namely the recorded video, workflow JSON file, private image address, and generated images, directly by email to lichuanzi@matrixorigin.cn before the deadline of November 4, 2024. After a successful submission, participants will receive a confirmation response.

#### Step 4: Evaluation and awards

1. Evaluation criteria:

   - Innovation and technical merit of the model debugging results and training outcomes.

   - Clarity and instructional value of the explainer video.

   - Quality and creativity of the generated images.

2. Evaluation process: The event judging committee will conduct a comprehensive review based on the submitted models, explainer videos, and generated images, and determine the final list of winners.

3. Results announcement: The evaluation results will be published on the official event page and major social platforms on November 8, 2024. Winners will also be notified by email.

4. Prize setup:

   One first prize: RMB 500 Neolink.AI platform voucher + one MatrixOrigin hoodie.

   Two second prizes: RMB 300 Neolink.AI platform voucher + one MatrixOrigin insulated cup.

   Five third prizes: RMB 100 Neolink.AI platform voucher + one MatrixOrigin sticker and folder set.
