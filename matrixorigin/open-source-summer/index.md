## About MatrixOne

MatrixOne is a future-oriented hyper-converged heterogeneous cloud-native database. It supports hybrid workloads such as transactions/analysis/stream processing through a hyper-converged data engine and cross-machine room collaboration/multi-site collaboration/cloud-edge collaboration through a heterogeneous cloud-native architecture. It simplifies development and operations, eliminates data fragmentation, and breaks the boundaries of data systems, locations, and innovation. Since its open-source release in 2021, MatrixOne has accumulated over 1.6k stars and 118 contributors.
