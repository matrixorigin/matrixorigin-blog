## Talk Topic

**The Observability System of MatrixOne Cloud**

## Talk Outline

- Introduction to the MatrixOne Cloud architecture
- How MatrixOne Cloud + Observability is implemented

## Talk Abstract

MatrixOne Cloud is a Database as a Service platform that supports multiple clouds and multiple regions. An observability system covering metrics, logs, traces, profiles, and alerts is the foundation for efficiently gaining insight into failures and continuously optimizing performance and costs. This talk will first describe the overall architecture of the MO Cloud observability system, and then explain how we address challenges such as monitoring across multiple regions and multiple K8S clusters, continuous profiling observability, and database tracing.

## About PowerData City Open Source Tour

To promote the development of the digital economy, China has implemented a series of major policies and initiatives, including the establishment of the National Data Administration, data factorization, recognizing data resources on balance sheets, and various open source development policies. These developments have also brought new opportunities and challenges for urban development.

Based on this, PowerData has decided to launch a series of events in ten cities across China in 2024 under the name "Digital Economy - City Open Source Tour," deepening the application and promotion of open source technologies in the digital economy.
