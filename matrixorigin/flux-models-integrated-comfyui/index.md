The Flux model was released by Black Forest Labs. It is an open-source AI image generation model developed by a team whose members largely come from the core Stable Diffusion development team. Released in August 2024, Flux has 12 billion parameters and is one of the largest open-source text-to-image generation models available today. Flux aims to advance generative AI by setting a new industry benchmark and providing both open-source and commercial editions to meet different user needs.

![flux](./images/f1.png?width=800)

Flux stands out for its excellent image generation capabilities and broad range of application scenarios. It performs especially well in portrait generation, accurately capturing subtle expressions and skin tones while supporting multiple resolutions and aspect ratios, breaking the limits of traditional AI tools. Flux also provides three versions: Pro, Dev, and Schnell. Users can choose the edition that best fits their needs. With these capabilities, Flux delivers better image quality and prompt responsiveness than Midjourney and DALL-E, giving users a more efficient and precise creative experience.

### Beginner's Guide

How can you use the Flux model through the ComfyUI image on Neolink.AI?

- Step 1

Go to the Neolink.AI official website at [https://www.neolink.ai](https://www.neolink.ai), then click the console to enter the login or registration page.

![flux](./images/f2.png?width=800)

- Step 2

Enter your phone number, get the verification code, and click the login/register button. You will then enter the console successfully. If this is your first time using the platform, registration will be completed automatically.

![flux](./images/f3.png?width=800)

- Step 3

After logging in, click **Create Instance** under compute instances. When selecting an image, find the image whose prefix is `comfyui-flux`. Developers can choose the appropriate GPU specification based on their needs and easily create a ComfyUI instance.

![flux](./images/f5.png?width=800)

![flux](./images/f6.png?width=800)

- Step 4

After the instance is created, you can start ComfyUI through the platform's built-in tools. The startup steps are as follows:

1. Go to the compute instance page on the Neolink.AI platform.

2. In the deployed instance, find the built-in tools option.

3. Click ComfyUI to open the deployed ComfyUI image.

4. Enter the prompt `a cloud with the word 'flux' on it`, then click **Queue Prompt** to start image generation.

![flux](./images/f7.png?width=800)

![flux](./images/f8.png?width=800)
