Ding ding! MatrixOne is participating in Open Source Summer for the third year. Open Source Summer is initiated by the Open Source Software Supply Chain Lighthouse Program of the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

We hope that this summer, MatrixOne can accompany students in the development and maintenance of open source software, help cultivate and discover more outstanding student developers, and attract more students to grow together with us.

Join the MatrixOne open source project to gain deep insight into database product architecture, AI, and large model application knowledge. You will also have the opportunity to work alongside MO technical experts and become a core community contributor!
