A truly future-facing intelligent transformation starts with true control over data.

The wave of generative AI (GenAI) is reshaping industries at an unprecedented pace. Gartner predicts that by 2028, at least 15% of daily work decisions will be made autonomously by AI Agents, and 33% of enterprise software applications will integrate Agent capabilities. This transformation is not a short-lived technology boom, but the "new normal" of the business world.

However, behind the excitement in consumer applications, the enterprise market is facing a more complex **trust crisis**. Research shows that more than 80% of enterprise AI projects stall after the POC stage. When AI Agents hallucinate or provide incorrect information in critical business scenarios, how can enterprises trust GenAI in core production, risk control, and decision-making systems?

## The Root Cause: Data, Not Models

The real challenge is not model capability, but the data foundation. The current state of enterprise data is like an iceberg and faces six core challenges.

Enterprise data is severely fragmented, and multimodal data integration is complex. Unified management is lacking, and requirements for parsing, governance, and enterprise data capabilities are extremely high. At the same time, most enterprises lack evaluation and feedback optimization mechanisms, making it difficult to capture interaction data and user feedback. As a result, Agents cannot complete a closed loop of optimization. In addition, there are scalability bottlenecks when moving from demo to production, with strict requirements for response, stability, and resource elasticity. Data security and governance challenges, together with complex technology stacks and talent gaps, make the deployment path for AI Agents even more difficult.

## The Breakthrough: A Data Intelligence Flywheel

As a leading data intelligence platform provider, we believe the real breakthrough is to build a data intelligence flywheel unique to your enterprise.

A data flywheel is a self-reinforcing intelligence loop. Its core logic is simple: first control the data and form high-quality AI-Ready assets; then use trusted AI applications to feed back into the data, continuously optimizing through user feedback and completing a closed-loop acceleration. Repeating this cycle ultimately accelerates enterprise innovation and business growth. Therefore, we believe this closed-loop system should consist of two major engines:

**Intelligence from data (Control Your Data)**: Break down data fragmentation through unified access, transform unstructured knowledge into AI-Ready structured assets through intelligent processing, and achieve version management, lineage tracing, and strict permission control through refined governance.

**Data governed by intelligence (Trust Your AI)**: Use trusted AI applications, such as RAG, to provide services, collect user feedback, and automatically route that feedback back into data processing, optimizing data assets and processing models.
