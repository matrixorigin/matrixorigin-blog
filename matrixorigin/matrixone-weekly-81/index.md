## ▶ Ant Group Appoints CFO as President and Splits International, Database, and Technology Businesses into Independent Operations

On March 19, Jing Xiandong, Chairman and CEO of Ant Group, issued an internal letter announcing a new round of organizational restructuring. Han Xinyi will serve as President of Ant Group, taking full responsibility for digital payment, digital connectivity, and digital finance businesses, while concurrently serving as Group CFO and reporting to Jing Xiandong. Ant International, OceanBase (the database business), and Ant Digital Technologies will each establish a board of directors and operate independently in the market under a CEO responsibility system led by the board. The adjustment takes effect immediately.

_Source: https://companies.caixin.com/m/2024-03-19/102177038.html_

## ▶ Zilliz and NVIDIA Release the World's First GPU-Accelerated Vector Database

On the morning of March 20, San Francisco time, Zilliz and NVIDIA jointly released Milvus 2.4 at GTC 2024. This vector database system uses NVIDIA GPUs' efficient parallel processing capabilities and the newly introduced CAGRA (CUDA-Accelerated Graph Index for Vector Retrieval) technology in the RAPIDS cuVS library, providing GPU-based vector indexing and search acceleration.

_Source: https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/a3Mdudv-xizGusWPjAHX6Q_
